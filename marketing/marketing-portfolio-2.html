01 · About Me

I'm a digital marketing specialist who plans campaigns the way an analyst reads a balance sheet — every channel has to justify its line item.

Over eight years I've run SEO, paid acquisition, content and lifecycle programs for a Series-B fintech, a twelve-property hospitality group, and a handful of D2C launches that had no category playbook to copy. I'm equally comfortable in a keyword-research spreadsheet and a boardroom defending the media plan that spreadsheet produced.

Career goal: to lead full-funnel marketing for a growth-stage company where brand and performance report to the same strategy.
