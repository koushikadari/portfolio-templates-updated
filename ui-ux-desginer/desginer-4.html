About me

A short orbit around who I am, how I think, and what pulls me into a project.

🪐

My story

I started as a front-end developer, got frustrated fixing designs that didn't account for real constraints, and crossed over to design so I could own the problem earlier. Eight years later I still write a little code — it keeps my design decisions honest.

🧭

How I work

Research before wireframes, wireframes before pixels, pixels before prototypes. I default to the smallest testable version of an idea, and I'd rather ship something plain that works than something clever that doesn't.

🎯

What I care about

Accessibility isn't a checklist item at the end — it shapes the layout from the first sketch. I also believe good design systems are a form of kindness to the next designer who touches the file.

☕

Off the clock

Long walks along the Marina, terrible amateur pottery, and reading interface-critique threads I have no business being this invested in.
