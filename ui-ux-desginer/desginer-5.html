About 01 / 08

Twelve years behind
the viewfinder.

Click to change photo

I've always believed a photograph should ask something of the viewer, not just show them a moment.

Based between Lisbon and Berlin, I split my time between editorial commissions and long-term documentary projects. My work has appeared in independent print journals and gallery shows across Europe. I shoot primarily on medium-format film, believing the slowness of the process is part of what the final image says. When I'm not on assignment, I run a small darkroom workshop for emerging photographers.
