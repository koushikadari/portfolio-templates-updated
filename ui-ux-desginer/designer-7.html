About 01 / 08

Fifteen years of turning
ambiguity into interface.

I believe good design is mostly good listening — to users, to constraints, to the quiet friction people don't always name.

I'm a product and brand designer based in Lisbon, currently working with founders and product teams to shape early-stage platforms into considered, usable experiences. My background spans editorial design, design systems, and front-end craft — which means I care as much about the 8px grid as I do about the story a brand tells. Outside of client work, I write about design systems and mentor junior designers entering the field.
