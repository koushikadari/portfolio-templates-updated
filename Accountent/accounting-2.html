I'm Sarah Mitchell, a Chartered Public Accountant with over 14 years of experience across public accounting, corporate finance, and private wealth advisory. My career began at Deloitte, where I developed an instinct for finding value in financial complexity.

Today, I operate an independent practice serving entrepreneurs, SMEs, and high-net-worth individuals who demand more than compliance — they demand insight. I combine technical rigour with a genuine understanding of my clients' ambitions.

I hold a CPA designation, an MBA from the Wharton School, and certifications in tax planning and forensic accounting.
