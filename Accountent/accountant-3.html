About me

I'm a portfolio accountant with a background spanning fund accounting, real estate, and investment management. Over 12+ years I've prepared NAVs and financial statements for portfolios ranging from a handful of properties to global holdings worth billions — always with an eye for the reconciling item everyone else missed.

My approach is simple: numbers should be accurate, timely, and explainable in plain English to whoever is reading them — auditors, investors, or the property manager down the hall.
