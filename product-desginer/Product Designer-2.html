The
Story
So Far

Design / Strategy
Branding / UX
Art Direction

I'm a product designer and creative director with over 8 years shaping how people interact with digital products. I believe great design is an act of empathy — every pixel, every flow, every word placed with intention.

My practice spans brand identity, product design, and creative direction. I've led design teams at funded startups, worked with global agencies, and shipped products used by millions of people across the world.

Philosophy
Design should be honest, functional, and — above all — human.
Approach
Research-led. Systems thinking. Execution obsessed. No decorative noise.
When Not Designing
Running, film photography, and arguing about typography in cafes.
Currently Reading
Dieter Rams: As Little Design as Possible — for the 4th time.
